# Glossary

**ATP (Around-the-Post)** — A shot hit around the outside of the net post, not over it. Legal and spectacular.

**Banger** — A player who relies on hard, flat drives rather than the soft dinking game.

**Bert** — An Erne shot performed by the player on the opposite (partner's) side of the court, crossing in front of their partner.

**Dink** — A soft shot that arcs over the net and lands in the kitchen. The backbone of advanced play.

**Drive** — A hard, flat, low shot hit with pace.

**Erne** — An advanced shot where a player jumps around or over the kitchen corner to volley from outside the NVZ.

**Fault** — Any rule violation that ends a rally (e.g., hitting out, volleying from the kitchen).

**Kitchen** — The non-volley zone (NVZ) — the 7-foot area on each side of the net where you may not volley.

**Let** — A serve that clips the net and lands in the correct service box. The serve is replayed.

**Lob** — A high, deep shot intended to push opponents off the kitchen line.

**NVZ** — Non-Volley Zone. See "Kitchen."

**Overhead / Smash** — An aggressive downward strike on a high ball.

**Poach** — In doubles, when one player crosses to take a shot that would normally go to their partner.

**Rally** — The exchange of shots between serves.

**Reset** — A soft, controlled shot used to slow down a fast rally and regain a neutral position.

**Side Out** — When the serve passes from one team to the other.

**Skinny Singles** — A singles practice drill played only on one half of the court (two diagonal halves), simulating kitchen line battles.

**Slam / Put-Away** — A decisive, hard shot intended to end the rally.

**Stacking** — A doubles positioning strategy where both players line up on the same side before the serve/return to maintain preferred court positions.

**Third-Shot Drop** — A soft shot from the baseline (the serving team's third shot) intended to land in the kitchen and allow the serving team to advance to the net.

**Transition Zone** — The mid-court area between the baseline and the kitchen. Dangerous to be stuck here.

**Unattackable Ball** — A low ball over the net that cannot safely be driven; requires a reset or dink.

**Volley** — Hitting the ball out of the air before it bounces. Not allowed while in the kitchen.
