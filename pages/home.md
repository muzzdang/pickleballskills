# Welcome to the Pickleball Wiki

Pickleball is a paddle sport that combines elements of tennis, badminton, and ping-pong. Played on a badminton-sized court with a modified tennis net, it is one of the fastest-growing sports in North America.

## Quick Facts

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Founded | 1965, Bainbridge Island, WA |
| Players | 2 (singles) or 4 (doubles) |
| Court size | 20 ft × 44 ft |
| Net height | 36 in at sides, 34 in at center |
| Governing body | USA Pickleball (USAP) |

## Why Play Pickleball?

- Easy to learn, difficult to master
- Low-impact — gentle on joints
- Social and community-driven
- Playable at any age or fitness level
- Indoor and outdoor variants
