# Hit Behind

A targeting strategy: aim to where your opponent is moving *away from*, not where they're heading. Good players instinctively follow the ball — use that against them.

## How it works

As an opponent moves laterally or crashes toward the kitchen, a lane opens up on the side they're vacating. Hit behind them into that lane. They can't reverse direction quickly enough to reach it.

## Reading direction

- Opponent moving left → hit right
- Opponent crashing the middle → hit the line they just left
- Opponent coming straight in → either angle works; pick based on their backhand side

## At the kitchen vs transition

Works best in the [transition zone](transition-zone.md) where opponents cover more ground vertically. At the kitchen, lateral movement is smaller and players reset faster — hit behind still applies but the window is shorter.
