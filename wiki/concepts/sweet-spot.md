# Sweet Spot

The center of the paddle face — the area that generates maximum power and the most predictable ball direction. Hitting off-center (edges, throat) loses pace and sends the ball in unintended directions.

## How to hit it more consistently

**Move your head with the ball.** Most players track the ball with their eyes but leave their head facing the target before contact. Instead, physically move your head to follow the ball all the way through the contact point. This eliminates peripheral distraction and forces focus on the ball-paddle meeting point.

The routine: ball approaches → head moves with it → see only ball and paddle → contact → pick head up to see result.

**Exception:** at the kitchen during fast hands battles, it's too quick to track with the head — focus on prediction and positioning instead.
