# Athena Pickleball

**Channel:** Athena Pickleball
**Coach:** Coach Jess (pro coach and player)
**Platform:** YouTube
**Focus:** Strategy, shot selection, decision frameworks, mindset
**Style:** Clean indoor production; mix of talking-head studio and on-court demos with an opponent; uses on-screen graphic overlays (text callouts, highlight circles) to reinforce technique cues

## Teaching Style

Coach Jess emphasizes **decision-making frameworks** over pure mechanics — her content gives players a read-and-react system rather than rote technique. Videos are structured around a clear problem ("you're choosing the wrong 4th shot"), a hierarchy of goals, and a simple if/then decision tree.

On-screen graphics are used well: text overlays highlight key cues ("TURN YOUR BODY / TURN YOUR SHOULDERS"), graphic circles draw attention to compact paddle positions. Studio and on-court footage are mixed effectively.

## Notable Content

- **4th shot decision framework** — four options keyed to what the 3rd shot gives you; strong block, roll volley, step-back drive, dink/neutralize
- **Beat younger players without hitting harder** — Coach Jess (48, competing at open level) teaches three strategy-based advantages: target the feet to force pop-ups; take time away with volley dinks and create space; control the middle to remove opponent angles and create confusion; none of these require extra speed or power
