# Briones Pickleball Academy

**Channel:** Briones Pickleball Academy
**Coach:** Jordan Briones
**Affiliation:** JOOLA (powered by JOOLA branding on court)
**Setting:** Outdoor private court, Arizona; also indoor facility for match footage
**Style:** Structured instructional with chapter timers ("Section #1 In: 0:10", "Recap In: 0:21"), live demonstrations with drilling partner, real-match insert footage. Topspin-focused coaching system (promotes topspinsecs.com).

## Teaching Philosophy

Jordan emphasizes an **offensive-first mindset at the NVZ** — every ball out of the air when possible, height-based shot selection, and maximum pressure without giveaways. Strong topspin focus; integrates real pro match footage to show concepts in game context.

## Notable Content

- **4th Shot NVZ Offense** — contact height decision framework; drive-drop counter strategy; three options against aggressive topspin drops; when to concede the kitchen line
