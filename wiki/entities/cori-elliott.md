# Cori Elliott

**Channel:** Cori Elliott (YouTube)
**Format:** CCC (Cori's Coaching Challenge) — live on-court coaching sessions with real students; also instructional breakdowns
**Style:** Real-play coaching with a student, not scripted demo. Identifies specific student errors, corrects in real time, and names the underlying cause. Direct and clear cues; low jargon.

## Teaching Style

Cori works by watching a student play, identifying the *root* error (timing, footwork, shot selection), and drilling that specific issue until it improves in live play. Her sessions show both the before (the error) and the corrected version in the same video. Emphasis on practical decision-making, not just mechanics.

Signature cues:
- **"The pocket"** — consistent ready-position contact zone in front of the body
- **Back-to-front weight transfer** — explicit cue for the drop shot (vs. arm-only)
- **Advance triggers** — don't go; wait for the read (ball dipping on drive, ball lower on reset)

## Notable Content

- **Transition zone fix** — advance timing on drives and resets; drop technique; foot behind the ball; the pocket
- **Punch volley** — backhand punch volley mechanics; door closed/open motion; locked wrist; elbow tucked; face angle by ball height
