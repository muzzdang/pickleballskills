# Cracked Pickleball

**Type:** YouTube instruction channel
**Setting:** Outdoor courts; recent footage from Pickles at Poipu, Honolulu
**Format:** Multi-tip listicle format with demo footage and slow-motion replays; covers strategy, shot mechanics, and pro-vs-amateur comparisons

## Teaching Philosophy

Cracked Pickleball focuses on **differentiating amateur habits from pro behaviors** and giving players concrete, actionable fixes. The channel uses competitive pro footage alongside instructional demos to show what elite play actually looks like, rather than describing it abstractly. Strong emphasis on one-two punch patterns, serve targeting strategy, and the mental shift from "hit winners" to "create situations."

## Notable Content

- **Pro vs. Amateur Differences** — paddle tracking (watching opponent's paddle instead of ball); responding to position with appropriate offense/defense; one-two punch speed-up targeting; third-shot selection by ball height; serve variety; crossing the midline in doubles
