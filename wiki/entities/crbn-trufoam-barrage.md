# CRBN TruFoam Barrage

A pickleball paddle from **CRBN**, a prominent paddle brand known for carbon fiber faces. The "TruFoam" designation indicates a foam core (as opposed to CRBN's standard polypropylene honeycomb core paddles). The "Barrage" name suggests a power-oriented positioning in the CRBN lineup.

**Brand:** CRBN
**Core:** TruFoam (proprietary foam)
**Face:** Carbon fiber (consistent with CRBN lineup)

## Key Characteristics

- Foam core paddles generally offer more pop and power with a denser, livelier feel compared to Gen 3 polypropylene paddles
- The TruFoam core is CRBN's proprietary foam technology — distinct from standard EVA or Thermoplastic foam used by other brands
- Higher power potential than polypropylene honeycomb paddles at equivalent swing speeds
