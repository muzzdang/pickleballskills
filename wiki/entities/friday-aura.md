# Friday Aura / Friday Aura Pro

**Type:** Pickleball paddle series
**Brand:** Friday Pickleball
**Price:** ~$129 (Aura); slightly higher (Aura Pro) — use code PBSTUDIO for savings
**Shapes:** Hybrid, elongated, and widebody (three shapes available)

## Feel Profile

**Aura**: soft and dense — very soft, firmly in the dampened/absorbed category; all-court power level; great for players who pop up resets and dinks; the most forgiving option in the soft-dense market segment

**Aura Pro**: similar profile but with more power; low-tier power category; Chris at Pickleball Studio considers it his #1 paddle of 2026 for price-to-performance

**Shared traits**: muted feel absorbs pace well; relatively chunky grip (4.25 in — noted downside for smaller-handed players); sweet spot is good out of the box without needing added weight

## Comparison to PaddleTek Honeyfoam

Both sit in the soft-dense category. Honeyfoam has more pop and power ceiling; Aura is half the price and plays in a very similar realm. For players who don't need the extra power, the Aura wins on value. For players who pop up dinks/resets, Aura is immediately the recommendation.

## Community Verdict

Consistently named as a top-5 paddle of 2026 by Pickleball Studio. Exceptional value at $129. Not the most exciting paddle, but reliably good across all shot types for a wide range of players.
