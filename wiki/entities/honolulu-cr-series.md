# Honolulu — CR Series

Honolulu's current top-of-lineup paddles: J6CR (elongated) and J2CR (hybrid). Considered one of the best paddles available in 2026. Now features Crystal Blue Endurance Grit.

## Key specs

- Core: EP foam center + EVA foam ring with notched "puzzle piece" edges + EP foam control joint at handle base (unique multi-density foam design)
- Thickness: 16mm
- Face: CFC layout (carbon fiber + fiberglass layer)
- Price: $195 ($175 with code KITCHEN)
- J6CR static weight: 8oz, swing weight: 112, twist weight: 6.24
- J2CR static weight: 8.1oz, swing weight: 110.8, twist weight: 6.65

## Why it stands out

**Low swing weight for an elongated paddle.** J6CR at 112 swing weight is "probably one of the lowest swing weights for a 16mm elongated paddle ever seen." This eliminates the common complaint that elongated paddles are too slow in the hands, while leaving enormous room to add weight.

**Adding weight:** Adding 8g total (lower corners + sides) can bring twist weight to 7.0 — very high — while only raising swing weight to 114.7. Still below most elongated stock swing weights.

**Predictable power.** Sits around Bread & Butter Loco power level — not the absolute peak, but very controllable. Doesn't sail balls long.

## Crystal Blue Endurance Grit

Honolulu's answer to grit degradation. Claims extended surface performance. Paddle owners without it can upgrade at a reduced price.
