# JOOLA Pro V

JOOLA's 2026 flagship paddle. Not a pure power upgrade over the Pro IV — a deliberate step toward softer feel, more linear power, and better soft game. Currently used by Ben Johns, Anna Bright, Federico Staksrud (Cosmos shape), and other pros.

## Key specs

- Core: polypropylene (Gen 3) — intentional choice, not foam
- Thickness: 14mm or 16mm options
- Price: $300
- Kinetic frame: thin carbon fiber in lower corners to improve flex distribution

## Shapes

| Shape | Character | Notable user |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Perseus | Elongated; most reach and power, less stability | Ben Johns |
| Scorpius | Standard; most stability and speed, less power | Anna Bright |
| Cosmos | Hybrid; middle ground, Federico's sig paddle | Federico Staksrud |
| Agassi | Elongated variant; slightly faster hands than Perseus | — |
| Hyperion | Perseus with arrow-curved top | — |

## Pro V vs Pro IV

| | Pro IV | Pro V |
|--|--------|--------|
| Power | Higher | Slightly lower (~0.5 mph serve speed) |
| Feel | Springboard-like | Softer, more linear |
| Soft game | Good | Better (drops, dinks, resets) |

## Core crushing

Still present (Gen 3 limitation). JOOLA adds carbon fiber reinforcement at the top of the head to reduce occurrence. Warranty: replace core-crushed paddles up to **3 times**.

## Kitchen Scorecard (Bobby, The Kitchen)

Dinks/drops: 9.5–10 | Drives: 8 | Resets: 9.5–10 | Net: 9.5–10 | Overall: **8.86/10**

Power tier: low-to-mid. Not a power paddle — that's by design.
