**Channel:** @liamduffin (YouTube)

## Teaching Style

Clear, principle-based explanations focused on decision-making frameworks. Emphasizes reading the *other* side of the net (opponent position) rather than just reacting to the ball. Direct and concise in corrections.

Key phrases:
- "Look at where your opponents are at first, then decide"
- "If you're forcing a shot, it's going to be most likely bad"
- "Give your opponents a quick little test in the first 2-3 points"
