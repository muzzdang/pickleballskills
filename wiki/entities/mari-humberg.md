# Mari Humberg

**Type:** Pro pickleball player
**Known for:** Highest slice-shot volume in the pro game; heavy reliance on backspin across all shot types
**Affiliation:** Appears at ThatPickleballSchool / PB Vision facility

## Playing Style

Plays an unusually high volume of slice/backspin shots — more than any other player Kyle Koszuta (ThatPickleballGuy) has observed. Slices returns in women's doubles almost 100% of the time. Adapts to fewer slices in mixed doubles against opponents who hit strong topspin thirds.

## Teaching Approach

Clear framework-based instruction. Identifies the root cause of errors (late contact, wrist usage) directly and corrects with immediate feedback. No hesitation in calling out common misconceptions.

Signature teaching sequence for the slice return:
1. Split step on server contact
2. Unit turn (full body, not just arm)
3. Meet the ball (go to it)
4. Contact out in front (locked wrist, face angle creates spin)
