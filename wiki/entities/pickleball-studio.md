# Pickleball Studio

YouTube channel and website run by Chris, focused on paddle reviews and recreational competitive gameplay. Known for independent, ethics-first paddle coverage and a searchable paddle database.

## What makes him trustworthy

- Commissions capped at 15% across all brands — no favoritism from higher-paying companies
- Actively asked Honolulu to *lower* his commission before his first review of their paddle
- Companies do not pay him for reviews — income is commission-only (via discount codes)
- Refuses to let brands use his quotes in ads
- Frequently ends positive reviews with "you probably don't need this if you already own X"

## DUPR context

- Targeting 5.0 via the "Chasing 5.0" series
- Peak DUPR: ~4.78 | Current: ~4.71 (as of Apr 2026)
- Competed in PPA Lakeville 5-0 division
