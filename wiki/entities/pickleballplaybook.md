# PickleballPlaybook (Austin Hardy)

**Channel:** PickleballPlaybook
**Host:** Austin Hardy (+ drilling partner Kieran)
**Format:** Structured on-court paddle reviews with defined test segments; also has a companion Pickleball Drills app
**Location:** Outdoor desert courts (Arizona-style setting)

## Review Format

Each paddle review follows a consistent structure:
1. Specs on screen
2. Power/Spin test (baseline drives)
3. Control test (drops, resets, dinks)
4. Firepower test (hands battle, pop)
5. Pickleball Drills app segment (skinny singles to 5)
6. Final recommendations

Reviews are not sponsored by the paddle brands being reviewed (disclosure given). Affiliate discount codes offered.

## Notable Reviews

- Luzz Cannon — max power Gen 3, ~$92 with code

## Notable Instructional Content

- **These Drills Fix 95% of Errors** — 4-drill consistency progression (drops, resets, volleys, dinks); game-to-11 drill format; learning order: control → consistency → location → power; ground push concept for distance control
- **MFWC System** — Monitor-Flow-Watch-Crash decision framework for transition shots and kitchen approach; Austin's core teaching system across multiple videos
- **Drive = defensive, Drop = offensive** — counterintuitive reframe of conventional pickleball terminology; drop advances serving team (offensive), drive gives return team time (defensive)
- **Straight-line / shin drop** — replacement for the classic looping kitchen drop; flatter trajectory aimed at opponent's shins
- **Zone-based grip** — hang off at baseline for power, choke up at kitchen for hand speed
- **FAST framework** — Fully ready, Aim, Shift, Train; hands-battle preparation system
- **DUPR critique** — Austin detailed flaws in the DUPR rating system and announced an alternative in development
- **Guest coaches** — recurring guests include Kieran (Dinkcredibles, D1 tennis, CSCS), Mini (5.6, 20 years coaching), Luke Larsen (5.5, 25 years)
