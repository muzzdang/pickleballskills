# Richard Pickleball

**Channel:** Richard Pickleball (YouTube)
**Affiliation:** JOOLA (equipment sponsor, films at JOOLA facility)
**Playing level:** Pro (competed at US Open, references matches with top players)
**Style:** Concise instructional with live demos; "Richie Recap" summary at end of each video. Uses a drilling partner (Zack) for demonstrations.

## Teaching Style

Richard gives tight, actionable content built around a central concept per video. Each video has a named structure (e.g., "Richie Recap" closing summary). He demonstrates with slow-motion isolation clips and live full-speed play. Strong emphasis on **simplicity under pressure** and **practical match application** over perfect technique.

Recurring themes:
- What to do when you're *not* playing your best (floor concept)
- Simplifying drives and drops under pressure
- Off-ball awareness (teases a series on this)

## Notable Content

- **Raise Your Floor** — mush drive → push drop for transition; push/slice + reach for net game; floor vs ceiling mental model
- **The Bump** — shoulder-driven forward push alternative to the slice dink; counters rollers; more pace and reach; went from 0% to 50%+ usage in 12 months
