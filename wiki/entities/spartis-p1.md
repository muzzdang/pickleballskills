# Spartis P1

**Type:** Pickleball paddle
**Brand:** Spartis
**Certification:** Not USAPA certified (UPAA only based on context clues in source); frequent backorder issues
**Distinguishing feature:** Longer-lasting grit texture; one of the standout paddles for durable spin surface in 2026

## Feel Profile

All-court to low-power range — not the most aggressive paddle on the market but not a control-only option either. High spin capability from durable grit surface. Well-rounded play across all shot types. Available in multiple shapes (took time before non-hybrid options were released).

## Community Standing

Named as one of the top paddles of 2026 by both reviewers at [Pickleball Studio](../entities/pickleball-studio.md). The longer-lasting grit is its primary differentiator in a crowded market — along with 11-6-24's Power Two and Honolulu's Crystal Blue Grit, it represents the leading edge of durable spin surfaces in 2026.

Main negative: persistent backorder issues and slow order fulfillment have frustrated buyers.
