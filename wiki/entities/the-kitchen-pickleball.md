# The Kitchen Pickleball

YouTube channel run by Bobby, focused on paddle reviews, serve speed tests, and pro tour recaps. Known for the Kitchen Scorecard — a per-shot breakdown rating system.

## What they do

- Detailed paddle reviews with Kitchen Scorecard (rates: drives, dinks/drops, resets, flicks/speedups, overheads/pop, putaway power, design)
- Serve speed tests with radar gun
- PPA/APP tour recaps with commentary (hosted by Chris Cali as "Sorry Not Sorry Pickleball Show")
