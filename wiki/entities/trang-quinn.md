# Trang Quinn

**Type:** Professional pickleball player
**Nationality:** Vietnamese
**DUPR:** 5.89
**Known for:** Described as the #1 Vietnamese professional pickleball player; high-intensity drilling approach; off-the-bounce speed-up follow-through work

## Playing Style

Trang Quinn drills with [Cori Elliott](../entities/cori-elliott.md) at competitive speed. Her drilling structure runs kitchen work first (straight dinking → crosscourt) then expands outward (resets → drops/drives → volleys → speed-up game → combo patterns). She emphasizes **timed drilling over score-keeping** to focus on pattern quality.

Her self-identified area of focus: "off the bounce speed-up and follow-through with the second shot" — attacking from the bounce and then pressing the advantage on the next ball.

Her signature reset drill: the off-kitchen player must get the ball to bounce in the kitchen 5 consecutive times while the net player actively tries to prevent it.
