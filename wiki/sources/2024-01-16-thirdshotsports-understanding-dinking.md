# Understanding Dinking (Third Shot Sports)

**Channel:** Third Shot Sports
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TUEp7IDU414

## Summary

Brief intro to what dinking is and why players use it. Foundational context for beginners.

## Key Takeaways

- Dink is a low/slow ball in or near the kitchen — not meant to win the point, meant to keep you out of trouble
- Forces opponents to hit upward (they can't attack a low ball hard and keep it in)
- At higher levels, dinks also become offensive — moving opponents around to force a high ball
- At its root: a neutralizing shot; both player and opponent are trying to wait for something high they can attack
