# Effective Grips for Pickleball (Third Shot Sports)

**Channel:** Third Shot Sports
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3pHRXYPKKek
**Duration:** 1:16 (short) | **Uploaded:** 2024-02-01

## Key Takeaway

For **drives**, the Eastern forehand grip (hand slightly behind the paddle) is preferred over Continental because it naturally closes the paddle face — keeping drives low and fast. Continental naturally opens the face, which can send drives high/long. Eastern forehand puts the hand in a strong hitting position for powerful, flat drives.
