# Overhead Smash (Third Shot Sports)

**Channel:** Third Shot Sports
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hnyPDMXoQXA
**Duration:** 1:06 (short) | **Uploaded:** 2024-02-03

## Key Takeaway

Get paddle up and into throwing position immediately when you see a lob. Then:

- **Deep lob:** get sideways (like a throwing position), use the hip rotation to generate power
- **Short lob near kitchen:** face forward (open stance) — going sideways would put you in the kitchen (NVZ violation)

Either way: paddle up quickly, take a full swing.
