# Third Shot Drop Grip (Third Shot Sports)

**Channel:** Third Shot Sports
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P5s4n_tzFk8
**Duration:** 1:40 (short) | **Uploaded:** 2024-02-03

## Key Takeaway

Two grips work for drops:

- **Continental grip** (V of hand on top of handle) — naturally opens the paddle face, useful for the arc needed on a drop. Works on both forehand and backhand.
- **Eastern forehand grip** (hand slightly behind paddle, like shaking hands) — more neutral face; requires conscious low-to-high swing to generate arc and topspin. Topspin helps the drop dip down at opponent's feet.
