## Key Takeaways

- The forehand putaway volley is the primary finishing shot at the kitchen — used when the ball is above net height and attackable
- "Finish points with confidence" framing: the shot requires commitment, not hesitation
- Likely covers contact point (out in front), compact swing, and follow-through direction
- Wrist snap at contact generates pace without a large backswing
- Placement matters — the volley should be aimed at a corner or at the opponent's body, not just hit hard
