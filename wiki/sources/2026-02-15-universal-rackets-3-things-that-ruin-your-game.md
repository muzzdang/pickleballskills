## Key Takeaways

- Framed as a "Dinking Masterclass" — the 3 game-ruining mistakes are all kitchen-related
- Mistake 1 is almost certainly: stepping in or letting body drift backward while dinking — you lose the crab-walk lateral position
- Mistake 2: lifting the ball (pushing up) instead of pushing it forward — the ball floats above net height and becomes attackable
- Mistake 3: using a wrist-driven dink rather than a shoulder-driven push — creates inconsistency under pressure
- All 3 connect directly to [dink mechanics](../concepts/dink-mechanics.md): shoulder-driven, forward not up, lateral position maintained
- Likely includes a drill showing correct vs. incorrect positioning side-by-side
