# Top 9 Rule Changes for 2026 (Cracked Pickleball)

**Channel:** Cracked Pickleball
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s9p4ABM_M7E
**Duration:** 15:19 | **Uploaded:** 2026-02-26

## Key Takeaways

1. No spectator consultation for line calls (must not, not should not)
2. Conflict between partners = ball in
3. Out calls must be made before opponent hits next ball
4. Ask opponent = they override your call
5. Serve must have clear upward arc, paddle clearly below wrist, contact clearly below waist
6. Multi-contact is now explicitly legal (multiple hits, continuous motion, not a carry)
7. Net post rule: ball bouncing on opponent's side then hitting post = they lose the point
8. 1v2 play: injured partner → remaining player continues alone
9. Visible ball in pocket = immediate point loss; object falling from pocket = point loss
