## Key Takeaways

- Comprehensive forehand drive masterclass — the forehand as a consistent offensive weapon, not just a reactive stroke
- "Low, hard, and unreturnable" framing: all three properties come from mechanics, not effort
- Low trajectory: achieved by contacting the ball below shoulder height with a level or slightly downward swing; topspin helps bring it down after clearing the net
- Hard: comes from [kinetic chain](../concepts/kinetic-chain.md) — weight transfer, hip rotation, shoulder rotation — not from arm swing speed alone
- Unreturnable: placement (wide, body, or to backhand) matters more than pace at the recreational level
- At 28 minutes, likely includes progressions, on-court demonstrations, and drills for building muscle memory
