# 99% of Players Hit Third Shot Drops Wrong (Cracked Pickleball)

**Channel:** Cracked Pickleball
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2joz3UhHI9I
**Duration:** 17:06 | **Uploaded:** 2026-03-13

## Key Takeaways

- Drop only when feet are inside the baseline; drive from behind the baseline
- You don't need heavy topspin — short swing, correct face angle, redirect the ball; natural topspin is fine
- Match pace inversely: opponent hits hard → soften; opponent hits soft → add pace
- Step back after serving to avoid T-Rex arm (return often comes at your feet)
- Don't sprint to kitchen after the drop — advance with split stops; use midcourt well
- Aim at opponent's feet/shins, give yourself margin over net; very short shots from deep court are low-percentage
