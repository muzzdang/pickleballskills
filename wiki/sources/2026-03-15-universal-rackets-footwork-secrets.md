## Key Takeaways

- Comprehensive footwork masterclass — footwork is the most undercoached element in recreational pickleball and the biggest differentiator at intermediate levels
- Key footwork areas: ready position (athletic stance, weight on balls of feet), split step timing, lateral crab walk at the kitchen, and transition zone movement
- Split step secret: step into your split BEFORE your opponent contacts — not after you see where the ball is going
- Crab walk principle: stay sideways at the kitchen, never turn your back — lateral shuffle keeps you balanced and facing the net
- Transition zone footwork: advance in stops, not sprints — split-step as the opponent contacts each shot
- At 35 minutes, the most thorough footwork resource Universal Rackets has produced
