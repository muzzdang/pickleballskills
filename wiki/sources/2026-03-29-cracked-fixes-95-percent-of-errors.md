# This Trick Fixes 95% of Errors (Cracked Pickleball)

## Key Takeaways

1. **Laser pointer theory** — point paddle tip at ball; shade slightly toward backhand side
2. **Seesaw effect** — paddle height matches court zone: hip at baseline, belly button in transition, chest at kitchen; doubles as out-ball indicator
3. **Shot selection** — aim at two spots over net (left-center or right-center); lower net, limits angles, more margin
4. **Split step** — split before opponent contacts ball; shuffle from that stable base
5. **Spin continuation** — use opposite spin to neutralize incoming rotation: topspin in → apply backspin; backspin in → apply topspin
