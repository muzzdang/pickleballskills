### Paddle Terminology Deep-Dive (Chapters ~25–43 min)
- **Swing weight:** Resistance to rotation around the wrist axis; higher = more plow-through, harder to swing fast; measured in kg·cm²
- **Twist weight:** Resistance to rotation around the long axis (off-center hits); higher = more stable, larger effective sweet spot
- **RPM / Spin:** Revolutions per minute of ball spin post-contact; measured via high-speed camera + ball tracking
- **Paddle shapes:** Widebody (more forgiving, lower SW), elongated (more reach, higher SW), hybrid (compromise)
- **Contact feel:** Polypropylene (crisp, feedback-rich), foam core (muted, more dwell/power), thermoformed (stiffer, more pop)
- **Swing weight changes of ~10 points** are typically perceptible to most players
