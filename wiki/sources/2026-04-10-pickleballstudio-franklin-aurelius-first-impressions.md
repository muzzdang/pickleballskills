# Franklin Aurelius 12.7mm First Impressions (Pickleball Studio)

**Channel:** [Pickleball Studio](../entities/pickleball-studio.md)
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5ic19mvc3Ng
**Duration:** 12:06 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-10

## Key Takeaways

- Ultra-light paddle: 92 swing weight stock — almost uniquely low; under 100 is very rare
- Niche paddle for people who specifically need ultra-light + small handle; most people should avoid
- Low plow through stock; sweet spot feels unforgiving; plays closer to all-court than power paddle
- **Requires weight to perform**: adding weight at sides (not head) dramatically improves feel; 24g at sides brings SW to ~104 while keeping it maneuverable
- Head weight raises SW much faster than side weight (12g at head ≈ same SW change as 24g at sides)
- Not Anna Leigh's current main paddle despite her signature on it; she uses original C45 hybrid / Dynasty
- Gen 3 core = core crushing risk long-term
- Best competition: Pickle Poppers Pop & Pink (foam, ultra-light, similar specs)
