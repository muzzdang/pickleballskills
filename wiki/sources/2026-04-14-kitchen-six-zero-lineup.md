# Which Six Zero Paddle Is Best (The Kitchen)

## Key Takeaways

- Six Zero Next Gem lineup: Coral (all-court, $200), Black Opal (power, 14mm), Ruby Pro (control/spin, polypropylene)
- Diamond Tough Grit on all three — 4× claimed durability vs. standard carbon fiber
- Coral: best for most players; all shapes available; gets better with light weight added
- Black Opal: for advanced players only; near-Boomstik level power; can get you in trouble
- Ruby Pro: for control players or fans of original Ruby; only Gen 3 in the lineup
- Decision guide: don't like foam → Ruby Pro; chase power + advanced → Black Opal; everyone else → Coral
