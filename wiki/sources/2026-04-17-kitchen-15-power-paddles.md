# I Tested 15 Power Paddles (The Kitchen)

**Channel:** [The Kitchen Pickleball](../entities/the-kitchen-pickleball.md)
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qFdAlcXEiDw
**Duration:** 28:20 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-17

## Methodology

5 serves per paddle with Pocket Radar Smart Coach; Franklin X40 balls. Average and top speed tracked.

## Results (average serve speed, ranked)

**Top tier (~57-58 mph avg):**
1. Luzz Inferno (MPP foam)
2. Enhance MPP Turbo ($100 — remarkable for price)
3. Six Zero Black Opal
4. Friday Aura Pro
5. Honolulu J6CR

**Mid tier (~55-56 mph avg):**
6. 11six24 Vapor Power 2
7. 11six24 Hurache Power 2
8. Selkirk Boomstik *(underperformed expectations — may be warm-up issue)*
9. Bread & Butter Loco
10. CRBN Barrage / JOOLA Pro IV (low-mid)

**Lower tier (~53-54 mph avg):**
11. Holbrook Fuze
12. JOOLA Pro V Perseus
13. Volair Shift Elongated

## Key takeaways

- Top to bottom is only ~3 mph average difference; 4 mph top-speed difference. Very zoomed in.
- Both top-2 performers are MPP foam cores — backs up reputation for high power
- JOOLA Pro V confirmed as low-mid power, consistent with its positioning
- $100 Enhance MPP Turbo in 2nd place — proves budget paddles can compete on power
- Selkirk Boomstik surprisingly 8th (possible warm-up effect)
