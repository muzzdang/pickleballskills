# Honolulu J6CR J2CR Review (The Kitchen)

**Channel:** [The Kitchen Pickleball](../entities/the-kitchen-pickleball.md)
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k6i-Ba_UCHY
**Duration:** 10:40 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-21

## Key Takeaways

- CR series (Core Reactor) replaces NF and FC Plus at the top of Honolulu's lineup
- Unique multi-density foam design: EP foam center + EVA ring + EP foam control joint at handle
- J6CR swing weight of 112 is exceptionally low for a 16mm elongated — fast hands + huge weight headroom
- Power level: ~Bread & Butter Loco — upper mid; very predictable, doesn't sail long
- Crystal Blue Endurance Grit: new extended-life grit technology; not yet long-term tested but early reports positive
- Pricing argument: longer-lasting paddles justify higher prices; $195 paddle lasting 12 months = same cost as $100 lasting 6 months
