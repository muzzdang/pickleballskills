# 7 Tips for Control (Cracked Pickleball)

**Channel:** Cracked Pickleball
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zqo16Pgd5SM
**Duration:** 14:21 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-23

## Key Takeaways

1. **Laser pointer theory** — point paddle tip at ball (shade backhand side); keeps you ready everywhere
2. **Head tracking** — move head with ball all the way to contact; same tip as prior video, reinforced
3. **Seesaw effect** — paddle height changes by zone: low at baseline, waist in transition, chest at kitchen
4. **Change your paddle** — powerful paddles hurt control for intermediate players; control-first approach
5. **Paddle face direction** — ball goes where face points; lock wrist on backhand dinks, loosen on forehand; grip tightness affects face angle
6. **Split step** — split before opponent contacts ball, not after; enables balanced movement
7. **Choke up for control** — higher grip = more face control; table tennis grip at kitchen for some players
