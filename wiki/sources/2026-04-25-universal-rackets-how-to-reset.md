# How to Reset Any Hard Shot (Universal Rackets)

**Channel:** Universal Rackets
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e6sSNUd2mRc
**Duration:** 16:24 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-25

## Key Takeaways

- Paddle out in front and away from body; paddle meets the ball, not the ball meets the paddle
- Elbow tucked to eliminate backswing — the most common cause of a big swing
- Square (neutral) paddle face for resets — NOT open like for dinks; open face = pop-up
- Don't flinch; get in athletic low stance so you absorb pace rather than getting pushed
- Redirect bangers with a low return — forces them to hit up, stopping the attack
- Progressive drill: start at 20% pace, build to 100%
