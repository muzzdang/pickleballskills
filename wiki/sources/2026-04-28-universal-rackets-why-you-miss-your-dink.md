# Why You Keep Missing Your Dink (Universal Rackets)

**Channel:** Universal Rackets
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xvOpeDGYUzo
**Duration:** 13:09 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-28

## Key Takeaways

- Dinking is a push, not a hit — shoulder-driven, not wrist or full arm
- Think forward not upward — push ball across net, don't lift it
- Lateral body position only — crab walk, never turn back, "cliff behind you"
- Paddle stays out front at all times — no backswing, no looping motion
- Open paddle face slightly to get ball up over net (only works with the forward push)
