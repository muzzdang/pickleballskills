# JOOLA Pro V Review (The Kitchen)

**Channel:** [The Kitchen Pickleball](../entities/the-kitchen-pickleball.md)

**Duration:** 9:53 | **Uploaded:** 2026-04-29

## Key Takeaways

- Pro V is not a power upgrade over Pro IV — it's a softer, more linear, placement-focused paddle
- New Cosmos shape (Federico Staksrud's sig) is the best hybrid option in the lineup
- Gen 3 (polypropylene) core is intentional; JOOLA is betting on Gen 3 feel vs. foam
- Core crushing addressed with carbon fiber reinforcement + 3-replacement warranty
- Kitchen Scorecard: 8.86/10; elite in dinks/drops/resets; drives rated 8 (low-mid power)
